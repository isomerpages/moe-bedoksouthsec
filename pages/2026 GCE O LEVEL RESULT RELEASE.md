<p>Dear 2025 Sec 4 Exp &amp; 5NA Students,</p>
<p>The GCE ‘O’ Level Results will be released on <strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2026 at 2:00 p.m.</strong>
</p>
<p>Please report to the <strong>School Hall by 1:30 p.m.</strong>, dressed
in <strong>school uniform / OneBDS uniform / half-uniform</strong>.</p>
<p>Students are reminded to be <strong>properly groomed in accordance with the Student Handbook</strong> (e.g.
no coloured hair, no visible tattoos, and no undesirable body piercings
or accessories).
<br>
</p>
<p>Students with inappropriate attire and grooming <strong>would not be allowed</strong> to
receive GCE ‘O’ Level results in the School Hall.</p>
<p>All students and parents are to be <strong>seated in the School Hall by 1:50 p.m.</strong> promptly.</p>
<p>Thank you, and we wish you all the very best as you take your next steps
forward.</p>
<p>Warm regards,
<br>Bedok South Secondary School
