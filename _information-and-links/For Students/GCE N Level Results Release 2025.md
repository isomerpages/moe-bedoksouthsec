<p>Dear 4N Students,
<br>
<br>The GCE ‘N’ Level Results will be released on <strong>Thursday, 18 December 2025</strong>.
<br>
<br>Please report to the <strong>School Hall by 1:30 p.m.</strong>, dressed
in <strong>school uniform / OneBDS uniform / half-uniform</strong>.
<br>
<br>Students are reminded to be <strong>properly groomed in accordance with the Student Handbook</strong> (e.g.
no coloured hair, no visible tattoos, and no undesirable body piercings
or accessories).
<br>
<br>All students and parents are to be <strong>seated in the School Hall by 1:50 p.m.</strong> promptly.
<br>
<br>Thank you, and we wish you all the very best as you take your next steps
forward.
<br>
<br>Warm regards,
<br>Bedok South Secondary School
