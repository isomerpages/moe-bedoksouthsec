<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>To provide training for students to play the game competitively
<br>To develop their skills to raise the level of competency of students
<br>To develop qualities of sportsmanship, leadership, confidence, teamwork
and fair play</p>
<h3>Key Events</h3>
<p>1. Inter-school Competitions (East Zone/Nationals)
<br>2. Various friendlies with schools through external competitions</p>
