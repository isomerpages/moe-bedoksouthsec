<h1>Malay Dance</h1>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/malaydance1.png">
</div>
<p>BDSS Malay Dance Cultural Club consists of an exuberant and exciting group
of students with a great passion in traditional and contemporary Malay
dance.&nbsp; It is actively involved in all school-based events as well
as public performances.</p>
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>‘Reach for the Sky’</p>
<h3>Motto</h3>
<p>The surest way not to fail is determine to succeed</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To exhibit a high standard of Malay Dance performance while displaying
graciousness, elegance, passion and multiculturalism through our dance
movements</p>
<p></p>
<h3>About BDS Malay Dance Performing Arts</h3>
<p>You will get the opportunities to showcase your dance talents via various
platforms such as SYF Dance Presentation, Chingay, Suria Mediacorp, National
Day Dinner @ Siglap CC &amp; VIA @ ‘My First School’!</p>
