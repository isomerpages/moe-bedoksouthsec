<h1>BIND</h1>
<h3>BDS Internationalisation and National Education Development Programme (BIND)**</h3>
<p>BDS Internationalisation and National Education Development Programme
(BIND) includes twinning programmes with overseas educational institutions,
Community Involvement Programmes (OCIP), and learning journeys to various
countries. This programme has provided our students with opportunities
to interact with people from various parts of the world so that they will
be more culturally savvy and attuned to the fast-changing global environment.
At the same time, students also enhance interpersonal relationships with
their own classmates, peers and teachers.</p>
