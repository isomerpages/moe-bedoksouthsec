<p>The BDS Parent Support Group (PSG) comprises like-minded parents from
diverse backgrounds who share a common goal to support and advocate the
school’s mission, vision and values through the school’s programmes to
enhance student’s well-being and learning, and to support students in their
education journey.</p>
<h2>Objectives</h2>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To support the school’s programmes through close partnership with the
school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To enhance communication between the school and parents to work towards
a strong school-home partnership</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To engage parents to nurture positive family relationships</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To establish a platform for parents to network and to share parenting
tips</p>
</li>
</ul>
