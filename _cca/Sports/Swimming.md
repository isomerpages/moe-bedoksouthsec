<h1>Bedok South Swim Team</h1>

<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2024_BDS_Swimming_Team.png">
</div>
<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>The Bedok South Swim Team comprises swimmers from diverse backgrounds
and experiences who have come together to help one another achieve their
personal best. The team has represented the school in the National School
Games competitions since 2017 and the swimmers’ hard work and dedication
have earned them places in the finals for both freestyle and medley relays.</p>
<p>Besides training together twice a week, the CCA also provides ample opportunities
for character development. Our swimmers have put their skills to good use
by participating in events such as Swim for Hope organised by SAFRA and
Changi Beach Club Charity Mass Swim to raise funds for charity. The team
has also come together to create a video to educate the school community
on various water safety measures.&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop character, mental and physical well-being in the swimmers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide training for students to swim competitively</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop qualities of sportsmanship and leadership</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To teach students to respect one another and work as a team.</p>
</li>
</ul>
