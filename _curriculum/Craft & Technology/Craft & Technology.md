<h1>Craft &amp; Technology</h1>
<p>The Art, Music, Design and Technology, and Nutrition and Food Science
units are the four units that make up the Craft &amp; Technology Department.</p>
<p>We aim to develop</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>a growth-minded individual who possesses 21st century competencies and
is well prepared to meet future challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>a creative youth capable of demonstrating understanding and sensitivity
to the needs of others while fostering an increased appreciation for art,
culinary creation, and design.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h1>Our Creative Journeys In 2024</h1>
<h3>ART</h3>
<h4>Secondary 2 Learning Journey To Design Orchard</h4>
<p>Students learnt about Sustainability in Fashion</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Understand Singapore’s fashion ecosystem knowledge and its impacts on
society&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>How Singapore’s fashion landscape shifting to sustainability and innovative
design</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Spark students’ interest and awareness of the feasibility of various career
paths in the local fashion industry</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design And Technology</h3>
<h4>3D Pen Printing Workshop (Collaboration with ALP Team)</h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills:</strong> By
overcoming challenges in 3D pen printing, students will learn to think
creatively and find solutions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Foster a culture of innovation and experimentation:</strong> 3D
pen printing encourages students to explore new ideas and take risks in
their design process.</p>

</li>
</ul>
