<h1>2026 Secondary One CCA Fair &amp; Parents Engagement</h1>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Objectives of the CCA Fair:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Help students and parents appreciate CCA as a key part of holistic education
and student development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build understanding of the LEAPS 2.0 framework and how it recognises students’
attainment and learning in the co-curriculum.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide students with opportunities to explore CCAs and make informed
choices.
<br>
</p>

<br><strong>Friday, 9 January 2026 (2:15 p.m. - 5:30 p.m.) </strong>

<p><strong>Attire:</strong>
</p>
<p>Students should change into PE attire after lunch for comfort and ease
of movement.
<br>Please bring along a water bottle to stay hydrated.</p>
<p><strong>CCA Experience and Selection Trials:</strong>
<br>CCA Experience sessions and Selection Trials will be organised for students.
<br>Details will be shared during the first week of school.
<br>
<br>Students who attend the trials will be given priority in their preferred
CCA during allocation.
<br>
<br><strong>Students admitted via Direct School Admission (DSA–CCA) need not sign up for trials, as they will be automatically allocated to their respective CCA.</strong>
