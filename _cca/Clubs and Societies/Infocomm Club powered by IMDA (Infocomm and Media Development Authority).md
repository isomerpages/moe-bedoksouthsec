<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>“BDS CCA: BDSians of exemplary CharaCter and Ability in Infocomm and Communication
Technology (ICT)”</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>“To actively engage and challenge every student in Infocomm Club with
the opportunity to develop into a responsible, confident and resilient
student who aspires to excel in the field of ICT, uphold the name and reputation
of the school through ICT skills and serve the community with the acquired
skills.”</p>
<h3>Objectives and Achievements (against IMDA 5 Stage Framework)</h3>
<p>Infocomm Club aspires to achieve the Vision and conduct the mission with
our sponsor, Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA), the statutory
board tasked to develop ICT skills in Singapore. In order to achieve this
objective, the Infocomm Club follows the following 5 stage framework of
IMDA, which is consistent with the Leadership (LE)- Achievement (A)-Participation
(P)- Service (S) - LEAPS framework of Ministry of Education (MOE).</p>
<h4><strong>1. Discover</strong></h4>
<p>Objective: Immerse in Out-of-Classroom experiences through Learning Journeys
to understand how Tech and Media are applied in the industry.</p>

</li>
